valorant



Valorant is a free-to-play tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. In the main game mode, two teams of five players compete, with one attacking and the other defending. Players use unique characters called "Agents," each with their own special abilities. The game combines tactical gunplay, inspired by the Counter-Strike series, with hero-based mechanics similar to Overwatch, and requires precise shooting and strategic teamwork to succeed. Gameplay overview Each of the 27 Agents has unique abilities and an ultimate ability, which can be charged by earning kills, completing objectives, and collecting orbs. Players use an in-game economy to purchase weapons and abilities, with money awarded based on previous rounds' outcomes. Available weapon types include sidearms, submachine guns, shotguns, rifles, and sniper rifles. While the main mode is 5v5 search-and-destroy, other modes like Spike Rush, Deathmatch, and Competitive (Ranked) are also available. Valorant is available on PC, Xbox Series X|S, and PlayStation 5. Recent updates and news In late July 2025, the PC version was updated to Unreal Engine 5, improving performance and reducing the installation size. The Valorant Champions Tour (VCT) continues, with teams qualifying for the Valorant Champions 2025 tournament. Recent qualifiers for the VCT Americas tournament include Sentinels, G2 Esports, and MIBR. A new map called Corrode was introduced in June 2025. Console versions for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S were released in June 2024, but without cross-platform play with the PC version.
